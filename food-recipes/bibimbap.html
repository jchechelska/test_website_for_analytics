Kimbap (비빔밥) recipe

Ingredients

  • mung bean sprouts
  • spinach
  • carrots
  • zucchini
  • eggs
  • steamed white rice
  • scallions
  • toasted white sesame seeds
  • toasted sesame oil
  • fine sea salt
  • grated or finely minced garlic
  • soy sauce
  • Korean chilli pepper paste

Directions

  1. Cut zucchini into slices and carrot into thin bars.
  2. Blanch each type of vegetable in hot water separately and soak them in cold water.
  3. Squeeze out the water and season with fine sea salt, grated garlic, toasted sesame oil, chopped scallions, and toasted white sesame seeds to taste.
  4. For zucchini, if you select this vegetable to add to your bibimbap bowl, the preparation is different. After you slice the squash, sprinkle with fine sea salt and let sit for at least 20 minutes to 1 hour. Squeeze out the water then do a quick saute over medium heat with the same seasonings as above.
  5. While that’s all happening make fried eggs, and prepare the rice.
  6. To assemble: Place the rice at the bottom of the bowl, add the vegetable toppings, eggs, and a few big spoonfuls of bibimbap sauce, mix, and dig-in!

Enjoy!
